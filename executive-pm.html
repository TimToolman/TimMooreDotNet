How I work

Principles I keep coming back to.

Principle

Customer-obsessed & data-driven

Principle

Scalable from day one

Services oriented arcitecture with redundant infrastruture. Design for failure before it's a fire drill.

Principle

Ship outcomes, not output

Roadmaps measured in revenue, NPS, throughput, and enforcement actions — not features shipped.
