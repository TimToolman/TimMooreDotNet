Storage Hound — Privacy Policy

Fetch your stuff. Keep your data.

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Storage Hound is a personal inventory app designed to keep your data on your device. This policy explains what the app does and does not collect.

The short version

  • Your inventory and photos live on your device, not on our servers.
  • No accounts, no analytics, no ads.
  • If you use AI photo analysis, that photo is relayed to Anthropic (an AI provider) to identify items; neither we nor Anthropic keep your photos for advertising or profiling, and we never see your inventory.
  • Subscriptions are processed by Apple/Google; we never see your payment details.

Data stored on your device

The following stays on your device only:

  • Your inventory — box numbers, names, notes, and item lists — stored in the app's local storage.
  • Photos you take or import are copied into the app's private document storage. They are not uploaded anywhere except as described under "AI photo analysis" below.
  • Your Anthropic API key, if you choose to add one, is stored in the device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

Deleting the app removes all of this data. Use Backup & Restore any time to export your lists and photos first.

Camera and photo library

The app requests camera and photo-library access only so you can attach photos to a box. Photos are used solely for that purpose and remain on your device.

AI photo analysis

When you analyze a box photo, that photo and a short prompt are sent for analysis and the resulting item list is returned to your device. Two paths:

  • Shared service (default): the photo is relayed through the developer's proxy server (a Cloudflare Worker) to Anthropic's API. The proxy exists only to hold the API key and enforce a monthly spending cap; it does not store your photos or item lists. A random device identifier (not tied to your identity) is used for rate-limiting only.
  • Your own key (optional): if you add a personal Anthropic API key in Settings, photos go directly from your device to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com).

In both cases, use of the Anthropic API is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy. The developer never sees or stores your photos or inventory. Nothing is sent automatically without you adding a photo.

Purchases

The optional "Storage Hound Unlimited" yearly subscription is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We never receive your name, address, or payment details. Purchase receipts are validated by RevenueCat (our billing processor) solely to unlock your subscription; see RevenueCat's privacy policy. Whether or not you subscribe (or renew), your data stays on your device and can always be exported.

Data sharing and export

When you use Backup & Restore (zip, CSV, or JSON export), the file is created on your device and handed to the system share sheet. Where it goes from there is entirely your choice.

Children's privacy

Storage Hound is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this location and the "Last updated" date revised.
