Principles

How good design earns the right to be on the screen.

Principle

Clarity over cleverness

Investigators clicking through a fraud queue at 3 AM don't need delight — they need to find the next case fast. The same rule scales up.

Principle

Prototype early, prototype often

High-fidelity in front of real customers beats Figma reviews with stakeholders. Use the prototype to find the wrong assumption before code does.

Principle

Design for the global user

If it doesn't work in Hungarian, Dutch, and Japanese on day one, the design isn't finished. Localization is a design problem, not a translation problem.
