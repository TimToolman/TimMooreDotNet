## Full description

FetchIt turns the "what box is that in?" scramble into a two-second search.

Whether it's the garage, the attic, a storage unit, or moving boxes, FetchIt
gives every bin a number and keeps a searchable list of what's inside.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Number and name every box, and jot a note.
• Add items in seconds, edit them inline, or move an item to another box.
• Search across all boxes and items at once — find "tarp" or "winter coats"
  instantly.
• Snap a photo of a box's contents, view it full-screen, add captions, and
  reorder your shots.
• Export your whole inventory as a CSV spreadsheet or a JSON backup.

SMART PHOTO ANALYSIS (OPTIONAL)
Add your own Anthropic API key and FetchIt can look at a box photo and draft
the item list for you — you review and edit before anything is saved.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account. No ads. No tracking. Your inventory and photos stay on your device.
The optional AI feature sends a photo to Anthropic only when you ask it to.

Light and dark mode included.
